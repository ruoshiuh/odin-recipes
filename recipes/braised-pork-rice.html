Braised Pork Rice

Description

Braised pork rice, or 滷肉飯, is one of the most popular dishes in Taiwan. It can be found in nearly every corner in Taiwan! This dish takes on different characteristics depending on the region. Each shop also adds their own personal style and twist, from varying levels of sweetness to unique spice blends. This recipe will introduce you to an easy, simple version of this wonderfully savoury dish, allowing you to recreate its comforting flavours at home. So let's give it a try!

Ingredients

  • Pork belly with skin 400g
  • Fried shallots 2 tablespoons
  • Water 500ml
  • Soy sauce 100ml
  • Minced garlic 5 cloves
  • Rock sugar 2 tablespoons
  • Boiled eggs 2
  • Rice wine 2 tablespoons
